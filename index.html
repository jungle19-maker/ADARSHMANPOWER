Experience Excellence
in Recruitment

VINAYAK OVERSEAS SERVICES is a licensed manpower recruitment and placement agency based in Una, Himachal Pradesh. We specialize in overseas recruitment, executive search, staffing, and skill development — bridging the gap between talented professionals and leading employers globally.

With our RA License (B-3393/HP/PER/100/5/11399/2026), we operate with full regulatory compliance. Our mission is to provide the highest quality of candidates to our clients and strive to exceed at expressing the services provided, ensuring alternatives in the hiring process.
